CHILDHOOD YEARS

           I was born in Manila and I have 2 siblings.1 I'm the middle child in my family.2 The oldest is a girl and the youngest is a boy.3 My earliest memory from childhood was my grandmother passing away when I was around 3 or 4 years old.4 I remember that day because it felt like I was suddenly brought into consciousness about all the things that were happening around me.5 I felt empty once my grandmother left us and ever since then I’ve lived feeling slightly abandoned.6 I don't remember her much but I know she was very important to me.7 My mom would tell me stories when I got older about how my grandmother loved me so much that she wanted to keep me as her own when I was just a baby.8 Maybe it was because my mom always told me how I was such an easy baby to take care of at that time.9 She told me how as a baby I didn't cry a lot and I was easily soothed with anyone who would take care of me.10 I guess I made people feel really excited when they see how I would respond to them.11 When my grandmother died, I was with my mom and we just got home from the wet market near by our condo unit.12 I really loved going with my mom to carry her grocery bags that's why I always wake up early when she goes to buy some food stock at home.13 Then when we got home, I just felt confused with the atmosphere and then suddenly in the bedroom my grandfather was crying and he just hugged me and told me that grandma passed away.14 I don't remember crying at that moment but when we were at the funeral I cried a lot.15 Everyone felt a big loss because my grandma was always admired in the family.16 My auntie, who was the youngest of my grandma and who is the closest relative to me, still misses her so much until now.17 Experiencing someone's care like my grandmother, although is very vague in my memory, I believe has influenced me a lot.18 That's cos her leaving made me very sensitive and learned to keep the people who will care for you.19 I really wished I had remember her more instead of just knowing her through pictures.20

TEENAGE YEARS

           My teenage years were a very rough patch.1 During this time I was feeling really depressed and isolated because of how my parents would have conflicts with money.2 That made me feel really insecure about myself that it made me lose the confidence to study properly in school.3 It was really hard to make any friends cos I was too afraid to get hurt if I got close to anyone.4 It's already really hard to deal with the problems at home so I didn't want to make my life more complicated with messy relationships.5 At the time of my junior high I was attending Hope Christian High School.6 It's a nice school but I couldn't fully enjoy it because of how afraid I was with everyone.7 I made some really interesting friends there and are still my friends til this day.8 Although to be honest we weren't the most healthiest frienship back then.9 Now we have grown so much and we're glad that we stuck with each other through many difficulties.10 When I had to change schools for my senior high though, I was very very depressed.11 That's because I got bullied and was socially isolated from the whole batch.12 I didn't really do anything wrong because I'm really quiet.13 However, people made rumours about me saying that I was ratting the whole class to the office of student affairs everytime the whole class was cheating.14 I never did those but they thought I did because I was in the girl scouts and I would pass by that office everytime I would go to the central office after school to bring down the Philppine flag after classes.15 I felt miserable but I couldn't say anything back because no one would believe me.16 So in the end I just had to bear it.17 One of the bullies did apologize for what they did cause they wanted to know from me if it was true.17 I explained to him what actually happened and he immediately tried to apologize and make the others stop.18 Unfortunately they didn't and everday after school I just cried.19 I used to cry so much that the corners of my eyes would bleed and when I wake up I couldn't open my eyes cause of the hardened tears.20

COLLEGE YEARS

           In my college years I attended UE for my first 2 school years.1 I was honestly afraid that things would be the same as my experience in senior high.2 and I had to brace myself again.3 I just had to go through this... is what I thought to myself but fortunately enough I didnt have to go through all of that again.4 My classmates here were less judgemental and now I’m excited to do stuff again.5 I am very fortunate enough to study something exciting everyday despite it being very challenging at times.6 I am always given support with everyone around me so I'm really happy at the moment.7 As a batch we would go out together after school hours.8 I'm really glad and so thankful for these precious memories.9 Being with them has healed me so much inside from the past.10 I'm grateful to them for giving my joy.11 Unfortunately, I will have to move to another country after my 2nd year ends in UE.12 That's why I try to make the most of everything with my classmates.13 I want to make my mark and become close with everyone here.14 This may be the last time I'll ever get to socialize with these kinds of group of people.15 That's why I want to not be so shy and afraid of them.16 And as much as possible document the moments I have with them so that I won't feel lonely when I migrate.17 I don't know what will happen to me when I migrate but I just hope that I won't feel misrable like how I was in senior high again.18 I hope that it won't be so scary and mentally challenging.19 I hope I can do my best and study to the best I can.20
